About Us

We are an independent, family run, neighbourhood restaurant located in the community of Crouch End, North London. We are passionate about sustainably sourced seafood and our aim is to deliver the highest quality food and service in a relaxed, welcoming yet vibrant environment.

Seafood fans will love our kitchen's offbeat and creative approach to ingredients. Oysters are firmly under the spotlight here at Lyon's, with a cach variety paired with a particular garnish in order to "amplify and compliment their specific natural flavour".

Our 62-cover restaurant keeps thing unfussy yet classily comfortable, with muted green banquettes and wooden floors.

Our Menus

Savor the best of Indian, Chinese, and Italian cuisine—all in one place!
From rich Indian spices to bold Chinese flavors and classic Italian delights, we’ve got something for every craving!

Indian

Indian Cuisine: A burst of rich spices, aromatic curries, and mouth-watering delicacies. Experience the authentic taste of India, from buttery naans to flavorful biryanis!


A feast for the senses! From the smoky flavors of tandoori dishes to the rich, velvety gravies of butter chicken and paneer tikka, our Indian menu is a celebration of bold spices and timeless recipes.


Experience the magic of India with every bite! From rich, creamy curries to crispy samosas and sizzling tandoori delights, our Indian menu is packed with flavors that tell a story of tradition and spice.

Chinese

Chinese Cuisine: A perfect blend of sweet, spicy, and savory flavors! Indulge in sizzling stir-fries, crispy spring rolls, and perfectly seasoned noodles.


Where crunch meets umami! Relish the balance of flavors with crispy Manchurian, sizzling hot and sour soups, and perfectly seasoned chow mein, bringing the heart of China straight to your plate.


A symphony of flavors in every dish! From the smoky aroma of wok-tossed noodles to the crunchy perfection of dim sums, our Chinese menu brings you the authentic taste of the Far East.

Italian

Italian Cuisine: Where simplicity meets perfection! Dive into cheesy pizzas, creamy pasta, and authentic Italian flavors that bring a taste of Italy to your plate.


A taste of Italy in every bite! From cheesy, oven-baked pizzas to creamy risottos and handcrafted pasta, our Italian menu is crafted with passion and tradition to satisfy your cravings.


Indulge in the art of Italian cooking! With hand-tossed pizzas, perfectly al dente pasta, and rich, savory sauces, our Italian menu is a love letter to comfort food at its finest.
